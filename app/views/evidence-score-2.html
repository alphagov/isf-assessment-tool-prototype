Does your evidence meet the criteria for a score of 2?

It also contains:

  • it includes information that's unique to that piece of evidence or the person (for example a reference number or a photo of them)
  • the organisation checked the person’s identity when they issued the evidence
  • If the evidence has any physical components, you must check they can’t be reproduced without knowledge or information held by the organisation that issued it.
  • If the evidence includes digital information (for example it’s a digital identity account), you must check it’s protected by cryptographic security features (such as a cryptographic key on a chip). You should be able to trust the evidence was issued by a real organisation when you inspect the cryptographic security features.
