Does your evidence meet the criteria for a score of 3?

It also contains:

  • it includes the person’s full name instead of their initials
  • it includes information that’s unique to that piece of evidence (for example a reference number)
  • the organisation made sure they issued the evidence to the same person who applied for it
  • It must also include a photo or biometric information (such as a fingerprint) that's unique to that person. If it doesn’t have either of these, it must be protected by cryptographic security features instead.
  • If the evidence has any physical components, you must check they can’t be reproduced without using software or equipment that belongs to the organisation that issued it.
