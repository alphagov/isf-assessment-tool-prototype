The strength of your identity checks

Low level of confidence

By doing the checks in the right way, you can have low confidence in someone’s identity.

This lowers the risk of you accepting synthetic identities or being targeted by third parties that have stolen other people’s identities.

Example

The Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency’s (DVLA’s) View driving licence information service lets users see information that’s not sensitive. There’s a low risk of identity fraud, so the service only needs low confidence in the user’s identity.
