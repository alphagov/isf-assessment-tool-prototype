Assess how your identity service meets the standard

Use this service to find out:

  • how well your current process checks users' identities
  • if your current process is at risk of identity fraud

This service is based on the UK government's Identity Standards, which help organisations decide the best way to check identity.

Before you start

You’ll be asked some questions about how you check users’ identities.

You’ll need to know what documents your process collects and how it makes sure if they’re genuine or valid.

You’ll also need to know if your service checks:

  • the user’s identity has existed over time
  • the user’s identity hasn’t been stolen or used fraudulently
  • the identity shown on any documents your service collects belongs to the same person that claims to have it
